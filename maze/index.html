Maze

Algorithm

Different algorithms can solve a maze and you can optimize every single algorithm afterwards to always find the fastest way. After the way through the maze was found, you'll see different colors. Red points mean the algorithm used this coordinate twice, green points are showing the final way through the maze and the pink points stand for the optimized way.
Java Basis Swing was used for the visualization.
