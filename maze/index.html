Maze

Algorithm

Different algorithms can solve a maze and combine them afterwards to always find the fastest way. You can also optimize every single algorithm. After the way through the maze was found, you'll see different colors. Red points mean the algorithm used this coordinate twice and the green points are showing the final way through the maze.
Java Basis Swing was used for the visualization.
