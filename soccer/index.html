Robot

I am part of the Bohlebots ToTheMoon team which consists of three members. I wrote the software in C++ and planned the most part of the hardware. While programming my focus was on high efficiency during the game. On the mainboard are 4 buttons which we use to change the mode of the robot. For example if the robot has kickoff or is in preparingmode / playmode. In April 2022 our team will participate in the RoboCup Junior German open soccer competition. After that the source code will be published on Github.
On YouTube you can find a detailed presentation and some game situations.
